You ask, “Why do I need to learn Hindi?”

Language is one of the most important parts of any culture. It is the way by which people communicate with one another, build relationships, and create a sense of community. Due to its vast number of speakers, Hindi is the fourth most-spoken language in the world. So many people, from children to adults, take a second foreign language at some point in their lives. They take a foreign language not only to communicate with millions of new people but for the cultural enrichment that each language has to offer.

With that in mind, following are the reasons that people may want to learn Hindi as a second language at their early age:

It prepares them to take high school and college language credit courses. It can benefit their brain. For example, there have been studies that being multilingual may help improve multitasking, make memory better, and can also help improve listening and hearing skills. It can also improve their career opportunities, as well as broaden their travel and cultural horizons. So, if you are ready to start on your Hindi language learning journey, come join Sri Govindaji Gaudiya Math Hindi School.
