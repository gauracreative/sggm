Seva Opportunity

Temples are embassies of the spiritual world, the place where the Lord agrees to reside and accept our service or expressions of love. The idea is that we practice our devotion to God and thus develop our relationship with Him.
Here at SGGM there are innumerable seva opportunities. Please contact us, and we'll do our best to accommodate your desire to serve the Lord.
